What is CSS?


A'ight, "CSS", Cascading Style Sheet.
Basically, this is what's going to decided what your page looks like,
font, colors, spacing, borders, backgrounds...
You name it, CSS decideds how its gonna look.

So, CSS works using a "Selector" and a "Declaration",
in normal people speak, you choose the thing you want to look a certain way,
then you say how you intend to change it.
Capeesh?

Example:

p {font-family: Arial;}

So, that "p", is the same as "<p>",
eveything between that tag will be displayed as simple old text.
That "p", is your Selector, anything that occurs between the tags will changed
as you dictate with your declaration.
So, the whole, "{font-family: Arial;}" bit is your Declaration,
the above declaration just controls font...baby steps, right?
