Lists

<ol>: Used to make ordered lists
<ul>: Used to make unordered lists
<li>: Entries in lists will be flanked by this tag
<dl>: Used to make a definition list
<dt>: Contains the term being defined
<dd>: Contains the definition of the term

Ex.1
Ordered List:
Loading a Sidearm

  1. Keep muzzle aimed at the ground/down-range and observe correct Trigger discipline throughout the procedure
  2. Check the breach for any unfired rounds (Remove if found)
  3. Insert magazine
  4. Firmly pull back on the slide, placing a round in the chamber
  5. Confirm that the saftey is disengaged
The weapon is now loaded and ready to fire.

Definition List:
Standard Daisho
Katana
Historically, katana were one of the traditionally made Japanese swords
that were used by the samurai of ancient and feudal Japan.
The katana is characterized by its distinctive appearance: a curved,
single-edged blade with a circular or squared guard and
long grip to accommodate two hands.

Wakizashi
The wakizashi is one of the traditionally made Japanese swords worn by the samurai class in feudal Japan.
The Wakizashi is shorter than a Katana and longer than a Tanto.
A very brief description, I know...it's just an example, gimme a break.

Just a quick heads-up, you can put lists inside other lists.
Just put another <ol>, <ul> or <dl> inside your <li> tags.
