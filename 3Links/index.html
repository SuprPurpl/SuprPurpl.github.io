Writing Links:
So, I'll place a link to Bungie's mainsite into the word BUNGIE.
As you can see (and hopefully test), the word Bungie will open Bungie's main site when clicked.(No new window, just straight to Bungie)
The line responsible:<a href="https://www.Bungie.net">BUNGIE</a>
So it's sorta like you've put a mask over https://www.bungie.net and disguised it as "BUNGIE".
You can also place mail links like Email Author
Clicking on that link opens your mail program and adds my email address as the recipient,
this is helpful for obvious reasons.
The line responsible:<a href="mailto:verakai141@gmail.com">Email Author</a>

Now let's say I wanted that Bungie link to send me to Bungie,
but to do so by opening a new window, I'd just code it differently.
The new line:<a href="http://www.bungie.net" target="_blank">Bungie</a>
Easy, right?
